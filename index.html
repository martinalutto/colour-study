Before you begin
How this works
1
Look at an image
You will be shown a coloured pattern for 10 seconds. Try to estimate what percentage of the image is blue.
2
Give your first estimate
Enter a number from 0 to 100. There is no right or wrong — just your best guess.
3
See the group average
You will see the average estimate of your group. You may revise your answer in light of this information.
4
Estimate again — twice more
This happens for 3 rounds total: one individual estimate, then two more after seeing the group average each time.
Important: please make each estimate independently, without discussing with others in the room until the session is complete.
